About Me

Who I Am

I am an aspiring engineer passionate about electronics, communication systems, and information engineering. My academic path reflects dedication and consistency, from completing SEE and SLC at Shree Adarsha Secondary School to joining the National College of Engineering.


Beyond academics, I enjoy programming (especially in C), structured problem-solving, and creating clear, visually organized presentations. I am also building my personal blog to share knowledge, projects, and reflections.


Music, creative exploration, and digital tools balance my technical pursuits, keeping me inspired and versatile.

About This Program

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Civil Engineering (B.E. Civil) at IOE is a four-year undergraduate program covering structural design, transportation, water resources, geotechnical, and environmental engineering. Students gain hands-on experience through lab sessions, field surveys, and a final project.
